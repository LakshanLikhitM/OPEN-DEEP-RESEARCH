A REPORT ON LANGCHAIN AND LANGGRAPH

LangChain:-

Helps you build apps that use LLMs (like ChatGPT).

Connects AI models with tools, APIs, and databases.

Makes it easy to create chatbots and assistants.

Has many ready-made components you can reuse.

Popular and beginner-friendly library for AI workflows.

LangGraph:-

Used to create AI agents that follow steps (graphs).

Lets you control the flow of an AI system clearly.

Good for building agents that loop, retry, or make decisions.

More structured and predictable than LangChain.


Helps prevent errors and makes long workflows stable.
